Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Yusin TV is a sports companion app that provides live match information, scores, and links to external streaming sources. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Usage data — screens visited, features used, session duration (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device information — device model, OS version, language, timezone
  • Advertising ID — for serving relevant ads (via Google AdMob)
  • Crash reports — anonymous crash data to improve stability

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and improve the app experience
  • To show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To analyse app performance and fix bugs
  • To send push notifications about live matches (only if you grant permission)

3. Streaming Links

Yusin TV does not host, store, or stream any video content. The app provides links to third-party websites that open in your device's external web browser. We have no control over the content of those external websites. Users access external content at their own discretion.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob
Ad serving & monetization
Firebase Analytics
App usage analytics
Firebase Messaging
Push notifications
Microsoft Clarity
UX behaviour analytics
Firebase Remote Config
App configuration

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for a maximum of 14 months as per Google's standard retention policy. Locally cached app data (match articles, config) is stored on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Yusin TV is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

  • You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings (Google Ad Settings)
  • You can revoke notification permissions in your device settings at any time
  • You can request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption. We do not sell your data to third parties.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use of Yusin TV after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
